Facial Landmark Estimation

The next step in the facial recognition process is identification facial features. Facial features include the mouth, eyes, nose, and outline of the jaw. The algorithm that finds your facial features is called face landmark estimation. This algorithm can be used to highlight your facial features in an image:

Face landmark estimation is a popular tool outside of facial recognition. For example, it is the method that Snapchat and other apps use to make filters that interact with your facial features.

In the facial recognition process, facial landmark estimation is used to do face alignment, which is used to correct for head rotation in the image.
